About Us

UTP Vida Media is a creative media company specialising in photography, videography, cinematography, and professional modelling services. The founder’s journey began in 2017 with Praiz Photography, a solo venture driven by a passion for capturing life’s moments. What started as a one-man operation grew into a thriving creative community. In 2022, the vision expanded and Praiz Photography evolved into UTP Vida Media. The company focuses on delivering high quality visual storytelling for individuals, brands, and corporate clients. Services range from event coverage and commercial shoots to cinematic productions and model portfolio development.
